Consent to Participate in Research

Basic Study Information Study Title: Use-inspired research on optimal measurement of inclusive teaching practices in STEM learning environments (NSF Proposal #2201928)

Principal Investigator: David Yeager, Associate Professor of Psychology, University of Texas at Austin.

Invitation to be Part of a Research Study. You are invited to be part of a research study. This consent form will help you in choosing whether or not to participate in the study.

What is the study about and why are we doing it? The purpose of the study is to investigate educational practices and ways to improve educational materials. We are conducting this study in order to study and improve classroom and faculty practices.

What will happen if you take part in this study? If you agree to take part in this study, you will be asked to complete online surveys and participate in focus groups to better understand the results from these surveys. You will also be asked to give similar surveys to students enrolled in your courses.

How long will this study take and how many people will be in the study? Completion of online surveys will total approximately 1 hour. Completion of focus groups and interviews will total approximately 2 hours. Approximately 50 instructors and 10,000 students will participate in this study.

What risks and discomforts might you experience from being in this study? To the best of our knowledge, the tasks you will be completing have no more risk of harm to you than what you would experience in everyday life. There may be a risk of boredom, fatigue or discomfort while completing the questionnaire. Participation is completely voluntary and may be terminated at any time.

How could you benefit from this study? You may not receive direct benefit from participating in this study. However, this research may contribute to improved educational processes in colleges and universities, which may inform your own teaching.

How will we protect your information? Your identifying information will be kept confidential, and researchers will not get access to it. Any presentations or papers published about the findings from this study will therefore not include your name or any other information that could identify you. We may share the data we collect in this study with other researchers for future research studies â€“ if we share your data, we will not include any information that can identify you.

How will we compensate you for being part of the study? You will receive $300 for your participation. Please note partial compensation is not offered; you must complete the entire study to receive the compensation. Additional opportunities for further collaboration will be compensated at approximately $50-75 per hour.

Your Participation in this Study is Voluntary. Participation in this study is voluntary. If at any time and for any reason, you would prefer not to participate in this study, you may stop at any time by exiting the survey of contacting a member of the research team.
